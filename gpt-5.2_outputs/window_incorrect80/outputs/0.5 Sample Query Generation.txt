% Query 1: Try to open w1 when it is explicitly not a window, closed, and not unlocked (should be executable; after the action it stays closed and becomes not open) (satisfiable)
:- query
    0: ~isWindow(w1) & isClosed(w1) & ~isUnlocked(w1) & isOpen(w1);
    0: openWin(w1);
    maxstep: isClosed(w1) & ~isOpen(w1).

% Query 2: Try to open w1 when it is unlocked (opening is nonexecutable if isUnlocked is true) (unsatisfiable)
:- query
    0: ~isWindow(w1) & isClosed(w1) & isUnlocked(w1) & ~isOpen(w1);
    0: openWin(w1).

% Query 3: Try to close w1 when it is explicitly not a window and not open (should be executable; after the action it becomes open and not closed) (satisfiable)
:- query
    0: ~isWindow(w1) & ~isOpen(w1) & isClosed(w1);
    0: closeWin(w1);
    maxstep: isOpen(w1) & ~isClosed(w1).

% Query 4: Try to lock w1 when it is explicitly not a window, closed, and not unlocked (should be executable; after the action it becomes unlocked and not locked) (satisfiable)
:- query
    0: ~isWindow(w1) & isClosed(w1) & ~isUnlocked(w1) & isLocked(w1);
    0: lockWin(w1);
    maxstep: isUnlocked(w1) & ~isLocked(w1).

% Query 5: Try to unlock w1 when it is locked (unlocking is nonexecutable if isLocked is true) (unsatisfiable)
:- query
    0: ~isWindow(w1) & isLocked(w1) & ~isUnlocked(w1);
    0: unlockWin(w1).
