% constraints/rules
% 1. Opening is nonexecutable unless the item is not a window, it is closed, and it is not unlocked.
nonexecutable openWin(W) if isWindow(W).
nonexecutable openWin(W) if ~isClosed(W).
nonexecutable openWin(W) if isUnlocked(W).

% 2. Opening causes the item to be closed.
openWin(W) causes isClosed(W).

% 3. Opening causes the item to be not open.
openWin(W) causes ~isOpen(W).

% 4. Closing is nonexecutable unless the item is not a window and it is not open.
nonexecutable closeWin(W) if isWindow(W).
nonexecutable closeWin(W) if isOpen(W).

% 5. Closing causes the item to be open.
closeWin(W) causes isOpen(W).

% 6. Closing causes the item to be not closed.
closeWin(W) causes ~isClosed(W).

% 7. Locking is nonexecutable unless the item is not a window, it is closed, and it is not unlocked.
nonexecutable lockWin(W) if isWindow(W).
nonexecutable lockWin(W) if ~isClosed(W).
nonexecutable lockWin(W) if isUnlocked(W).

% 8. Locking causes the item to be unlocked.
lockWin(W) causes isUnlocked(W).

% 9. Locking causes the item to be not locked.
lockWin(W) causes ~isLocked(W).

% 10. Unlocking is nonexecutable unless the item is not a window and it is not locked.
nonexecutable unlockWin(W) if isWindow(W).
nonexecutable unlockWin(W) if isLocked(W).

% 11. Unlocking causes the item to be locked.
unlockWin(W) causes isLocked(W).

% 12. Unlocking causes the item to be not unlocked.
unlockWin(W) causes ~isUnlocked(W).

% query
:- query
    0: isOpen(w1) & isUnlocked(w1) &
       isClosed(w2) & isUnlocked(w2) &
       isClosed(w3) & isLocked(w3) &
       isOpen(w4) & isUnlocked(w4) &
       isClosed(w5) & isUnlocked(w5) &
       isOpen(w6) & isUnlocked(w6) &
       isClosed(w7) & isLocked(w7) &
       isOpen(w8) & isUnlocked(w8) &
       isClosed(w9) & isUnlocked(w9) &
       isClosed(w10) & isLocked(w10) &
       isOpen(w11) & isUnlocked(w11);
    maxstep: isLocked(w1) &
             isOpen(w3) &
             isLocked(w4) &
             isOpen(w5) &
             isClosed(w6) &
             isOpen(w7) &
             isLocked(w8) &
             isOpen(w9) &
             isOpen(w10) &
             isClosed(w11).
