```
Actions:
board(floor, passenger) - a passenger boards the elevator at a floor (intended to be their origin).
depart(floor, passenger) - a passenger departs the elevator at a floor (intended to be their destination).
up(floor, floor) - the elevator moves up from one floor to another.
down(floor, floor) - the elevator moves down from one floor to another.

Constants:
at(floor) - (boolean inertial fluent) the elevator is at floor F.
origin(passenger) - (inertial fluent) the origin floor of passenger P.
dest(passenger) - (inertial fluent) the destination floor of passenger P.
passengerFlag(passenger) - (boolean inertial fluent) whether P is marked as a passenger.
boarded(passenger) - (boolean inertial fluent) whether P is marked as boarded.
served(passenger) - (boolean inertial fluent) whether P is marked as served.
above(floor, floor) - (boolean rigid fluent) floor F1 is above floor F2 (relative ordering).
validFloor(floor) - (boolean rigid fluent) F is a valid floor.

BC+ Signature:
:- sorts
    floor;
    passenger.

    % floors and passengers should be listed/added for a particular instance

:- variables
```
