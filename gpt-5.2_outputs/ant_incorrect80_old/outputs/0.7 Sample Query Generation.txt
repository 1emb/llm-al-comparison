% Query 1: Simple move from p1 to p2 when adjacent and destination is not clear (satisfiable)
:- query
    0: at(ant1,p1) & ~at(ant1,p2) & adjacent(p1,p2) & adjacent(p2,p1) & ~isClear(p2) & ~hasFood(ant1) & ~hasFoodAt(p1) & ~hasFoodAt(p2) & ~isNest(p1) & ~isNest(p2);
    0: move(ant1,p1,p2);
    maxstep: at(ant1,p2) & ~at(ant1,p1).

% Query 2: Attempt to move when the destination is clear (should be blocked by nonexecutable) (unsatisfiable)
:- query
    0: at(ant1,p1) & ~at(ant1,p2) & adjacent(p1,p2) & adjacent(p2,p1) & isClear(p2) & ~hasFood(ant1) & ~hasFoodAt(p1) & ~hasFoodAt(p2) & ~isNest(p1) & ~isNest(p2);
    0: move(ant1,p1,p2).

% Query 3: Pick up food at p1 when ant is at p1, food is there, and ant does not already have food (satisfiable)
:- query
    0: at(ant1,p1) & ~at(ant1,p2) & adjacent(p1,p2) & adjacent(p2,p1) & ~isClear(p1) & ~isClear(p2) & hasFoodAt(p1) & ~hasFoodAt(p2) & ~hasFood(ant1) & ~isNest(p1) & ~isNest(p2);
    0: pickUpFood(ant1,p1);
    maxstep: hasFood(ant1) & ~hasFoodAt(p1).

% Query 4: Drop food at a nest when ant is at the nest and has food (satisfiable)
:- query
    0: at(ant1,p1) & ~at(ant1,p2) & adjacent(p1,p2) & adjacent(p2,p1) & ~isClear(p1) & ~isClear(p2) & isNest(p1) & ~isNest(p2) & hasFood(ant1) & ~hasFoodAt(p1) & ~hasFoodAt(p2);
    0: dropFoodAtNest(ant1,p1);
    maxstep: ~hasFood(ant1) & hasFoodAt(p1).

% Query 5: Try to execute two different move actions for the same ant at the same time (blocked by concurrency constraint) (unsatisfiable)
:- query
    0: at(ant1,p1) & ~at(ant1,p2) & adjacent(p1,p2) & adjacent(p2,p1) & ~isClear(p1) & ~isClear(p2) & ~hasFood(ant1) & ~hasFoodAt(p1) & ~hasFoodAt(p2) & ~isNest(p1) & ~isNest(p2);
    0: move(ant1,p1,p2) & move(ant1,p2,p1).
