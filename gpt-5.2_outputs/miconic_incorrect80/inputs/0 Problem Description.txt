This domain describes an elevator system in which people may have an origin floor and a destination floor, floors may be related by an “above” relation, the elevator can be located at a floor, and people can be marked as having boarded or having been served. The boarding action can occur only when the referenced floor is indeed a floor, the referenced person is not a passenger, the elevator is not at that floor, and the person is not recorded as having that floor as their origin; when it happens, it removes the fact that the person has boarded. The departing action can occur only when the referenced floor is not a floor, the referenced person is not a passenger, the elevator is not at that floor, the person is not recorded as having that floor as their destination, and the person is already marked as boarded; when it happens, it makes the person boarded and removes the fact that the person has been served. The upward movement action can occur only when neither of the two referenced floors is a floor, the elevator is at the first referenced floor, and the first referenced floor is not above the second; when it happens, it removes the elevator’s presence at the second referenced floor and asserts the elevator’s presence at the first referenced floor. The downward movement action can occur only when neither referenced floor is a floor, the elevator is not at the first referenced floor, and the second referenced floor is above the first; when it happens, it removes the elevator’s presence at the second referenced floor and asserts the elevator’s presence at the first referenced floor.
