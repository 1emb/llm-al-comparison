This domain models an ant moving among places, where some places may be adjacent, may contain food, may be a nest, and may or may not be clear, and the ant itself may or may not be carrying food. The move action can occur when the ant is at a starting place, that starting place is adjacent to a destination place, and the destination place is not clear; after moving, the ant is stated to be at both the starting place and the destination place. The pick-up-food action can occur when the ant is not at a place, that place has food, and the ant is not already carrying food; after picking up, the ant is stated to be carrying food and the place is still stated to have food. The drop-food-at-nest action can occur when the ant is not at a place, that place is a nest, and the ant is carrying food; after dropping, the ant is still stated to be carrying food and the nest place is stated to have food. The clear-obstacle action can occur when the ant is not at a starting place, that starting place is adjacent to a blocked place, and the blocked place is not clear; after performing it, the blocked place becomes not clear by removing the fact that it is clear.
