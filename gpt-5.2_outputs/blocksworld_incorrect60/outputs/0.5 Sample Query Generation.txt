% Query 1: Try to pick up a clear block that is NOT on the table while the hand is NOT empty (satisfiable)
:- query
    0: ~handEmpty
       & loc(b1)=b5 & ~onTable(b1) & clear(b1)
       & onTable(b5) & loc(b5)=table
       & onTable(b2) & loc(b2)=table & clear(b2);
    0: pickup(b1).

% Query 2: Try to pick up a block that is on the table (should be disallowed by nonexecutable pickup(B) if onTable(B)) (unsatisfiable)
:- query
    0: ~handEmpty
       & onTable(b3) & loc(b3)=table & clear(b3)
       & onTable(b5) & loc(b5)=table;
    0: pickup(b3).

% Query 3: Try to put down a block that is not being held (should be disallowed by nonexecutable putdown(B) if ~holding(B)) (unsatisfiable)
:- query
    0: handEmpty
       & ~holding(b4)
       & onTable(b2) & loc(b2)=table & clear(b2);
    0: putdown(b4).

% Query 4: Stack onto a destination block that is not clear (should be disallowed by nonexecutable stack(B1,B2) if ~clear(B2)) (unsatisfiable)

       & loc(b4)=b2 & ~onTable(b4) & clear(b4)
       & loc(b2)=table & onTable(b2) & ~clear(b2);
