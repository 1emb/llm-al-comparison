Actions:
board(passenger,floor)   - Passenger P boards the elevator at floor F.
depart(passenger,floor)  - Passenger P departs the elevator at floor F.
moveUp(floor,floor)      - Elevator moves up from floor F1 to floor F2.
moveDown(floor,floor)    - Elevator moves down from floor F1 to floor F2.

Constants:
isFloor(floor)           - Whether a given object of sort floor is a valid floor (inertial fluent).
at(floor)                - Elevator is at floor F (inertial fluent).
above(floor,floor)       - Floor F1 is above floor F2 (inertial fluent; could also be rigid, but inertial is safe for now).
origin(passenger)        - The origin floor of passenger P (inertial fluent).
dest(passenger)          - The destination floor of passenger P (inertial fluent).
passengerFlag(passenger) - Whether P is marked as a passenger (inertial fluent).
boarded(passenger)       - Whether P is marked as boarded (inertial fluent).
served(passenger)        - Whether P is marked as served (inertial fluent).
