% Query 1: Pick up food at p2 when the ant is at p2, p2 has food, and the ant is not carrying food. (satisfiable)
:- query
    0: loc(ant1)=p2 & adjacent(p1,p2) & adjacent(p2,p1) & clear(p1) & clear(p2) & isNest(p1) & hasFood(p2) & ~carryingFood(ant1);
    0: pickUpFood(ant1,p2).

% Query 2: Try to pick up food at p2 when p2 has no food. (unsatisfiable)
:- query
    0: loc(ant1)=p2 & adjacent(p1,p2) & adjacent(p2,p1) & clear(p1) & clear(p2) & isNest(p1) & ~hasFood(p2) & ~carryingFood(ant1);
    0: pickUpFood(ant1,p2).

% Query 3: Drop food at the nest p1 when the ant is at p1, p1 is a nest, and the ant is carrying food; afterwards the nest has food. (satisfiable)
:- query
    0: loc(ant1)=p1 & adjacent(p1,p2) & adjacent(p2,p1) & clear(p1) & clear(p2) & isNest(p1) & ~hasFood(p1) & carryingFood(ant1);
    0: dropFoodAtNest(ant1,p1);
    maxstep: hasFood(p1) & ~carryingFood(ant1).

% Query 4: Try to drop food at p2 even though p2 is not a nest. (unsatisfiable)
:- query
    0: loc(ant1)=p2 & adjacent(p1,p2) & adjacent(p2,p1) & clear(p1) & clear(p2) & isNest(p1) & ~isNest(p2) & carryingFood(ant1);
    0: dropFoodAtNest(ant1,p2).

% Query 5: Clear an obstacle at p2 from p1 when p2 is not clear and p1 is not adjacent to p2; afterwards p2 is clear. (satisfiable)
:- query
    0: loc(ant1)=p1 & ~adjacent(p1,p2) & ~adjacent(p2,p1) & clear(p1) & ~clear(p2) & isNest(p1) & ~carryingFood(ant1);
    0: clearObstacle(ant1,p1,p2);
    maxstep: clear(p2).
