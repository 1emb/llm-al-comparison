This domain describes an agent moving among places, where places may be adjacent to one another, may be clear or not clear, may contain food, and may be a nest, and where the agent may or may not have food. The move action can occur only when the agent is currently at a starting place, the starting place is not adjacent to the destination place, and the destination place is clear; after performing the move, the agent is no longer at the starting place and is also no longer at the destination place. The pick-up-food action can occur only when the agent is at a place, that place does not have food, and the agent does not have food; after performing it, the agent no longer has food and the place no longer has food. The drop-food-at-nest action can occur only when the agent is at a place, that place is a nest, and the agent does not have food; after performing it, the agent no longer has food and the place no longer has food. The clear-obstacle action can occur only when the agent is at a place, that place is not adjacent to a blocked place, and the blocked place is not clear; after performing it, the blocked place is no longer clear.
