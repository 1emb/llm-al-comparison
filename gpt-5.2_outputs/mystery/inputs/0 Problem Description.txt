This domain talks about entities that can be considered provinces and planets, a global condition of harmony that either holds or does not, a condition of pain that can hold for particular entities, and a directed craving relation from one entity to another. An attack can occur only when the chosen entity is both a province and a planet and harmony currently holds; performing the attack causes that entity to end up in pain, and it also removes the facts that it is a province and that it is a planet, and it eliminates harmony. A succumb can occur whenever a chosen entity is in pain; after succumbing, that entity is no longer in pain, it becomes a province, it becomes a planet, and harmony is established. An overcome can occur when some other entity is a province and the acting entity is in pain; after overcoming, harmony is established, the acting entity becomes a province, the acting entity comes to crave the other province, the other entity ceases to be a province, and the acting entity is no longer in pain. A feast can occur when an entity craves another, the craving entity is a province, and harmony holds; after feasting, the craving entity ends up in pain, the craved entity becomes a province, the craving relation between them is removed, the craving entity ceases to be a province, and harmony is removed.
