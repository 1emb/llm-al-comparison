This domain models an ant moving among places that may be adjacent, may contain food, may be a nest, and may be marked as clear, while the ant may or may not be described as having food. The move action can occur only when the ant is not at the stated starting place, the stated starting place is adjacent to the stated destination, and the destination is not clear; after the move, the ant becomes at the stated starting place and is no longer at the stated destination. The pick-up-food action can occur only when the ant is not at the specified place, that place has food, and the ant has food; after it happens, the ant no longer has food and the place is made to have food. The drop-food-at-nest action can occur only when the ant is not at the specified place, that place is a nest, and the ant does not have food; after it happens, the ant is made to have food and the place is made to no longer have food. The clear-obstacle action can occur only when the ant is not at the stated starting place, that starting place is adjacent to the blocked place, and the blocked place is clear; after it happens, the blocked place is no longer clear.
