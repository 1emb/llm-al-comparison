This domain tracks whether things are provinces or planets, whether there is harmony, whether a particular thing is in pain, and whether one thing craves another. An attack can be carried out on something only if it is currently a province, it is not a planet, and harmony is absent; after the attack, that thing becomes in pain, it stops being a province, it becomes a planet, and harmony becomes present. To succumb, the only requirement is that the chosen thing is not already in pain; after succumbing, that thing becomes a province, harmony is removed, that thing becomes a planet, and that thing becomes in pain. To overcome, it must be the case that the second chosen thing is not a province and that the first chosen thing is in pain; after overcoming, harmony is removed, the first thing becomes a province, any existing craving of the first thing for the second is removed, the second thing is made not to be a province, and the first thing is (still) in pain. To feast, it must be the case that the first thing does not currently crave the second, that the first thing is a province, and that harmony is absent; after feasting, the first thing is no longer in pain, the second thing is made not to be a province, the first thing comes to crave the second, the first thing stops being a province, and harmony is removed.
