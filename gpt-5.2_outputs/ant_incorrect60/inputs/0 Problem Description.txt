This domain models an ant moving among places that may be adjacent to one another, may be clear or not clear, may contain food or not, and may be a nest, while the ant itself may or may not be marked as having food. The move action can occur when the ant is not at the stated starting place, the stated starting place is adjacent to the stated destination, and the destination is not clear; after it happens, the ant is recorded as being at both the stated starting place and the stated destination. The pick-up-food action can occur when the ant is at a place, that place does not have food, and the ant is marked as having food; after it happens, the ant is no longer marked as having food and the place is also changed so that it no longer has food. The drop-food-at-nest action can occur when the ant is not at a place, that place is a nest, and the ant is not marked as having food; after it happens, the ant becomes marked as having food and the nest place is changed so that it has food. The clear-obstacle action can occur when the ant is not at the stated starting place, that starting place is adjacent to a blocked place, and the blocked place is clear; after it happens, the blocked place is changed so that it is no longer clear.
