Actions:
move(disk, loc) - move a disk to a destination location (either a peg or another disk).

Constants:
on(disk) - the current location (peg or disk) that the disk is on; inertial because it persists unless moved.
weight(disk) - the weight (or size) of a disk; inertial because it is an intrinsic property that does not change.
move(disk, loc) - exogenous action of moving a disk.
dest(disk, loc) - (optional) not needed since destination is already an argument of move/2.

    loc >> disk;
    loc >> peg.

    L, L1, L2, L3, L4 :: loc;
    N, N1, N2, N3, N4 :: integer;
    Bool, Bool1, Bool2, Bool3, Bool4 :: boolean.
