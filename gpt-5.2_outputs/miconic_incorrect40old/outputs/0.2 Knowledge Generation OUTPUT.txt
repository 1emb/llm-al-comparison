
% A passenger's origin floor and destination floor do not change unless explicitly changed.
% The lift's current location does not change unless explicitly changed.
% The above relation between floors never changes.

% Boarding at floor F by passenger P is only executable when:
% - the lift is located at F,
% - P's origin is F,
% - F is not considered a floor,
% - P is not considered a passenger.
% When boarding happens, it removes the condition that P is boarded.

% Departing at floor F by passenger P is only executable when:
% - the lift is located at F,
% - P's destination is F,
% - F is not considered a floor,
% - P is considered a passenger,
% - P is not boarded.
% When departing happens, it removes the condition that P is boarded.
% When departing happens, it also removes the condition that P is served.

% Moving up from floor F1 to floor F2 is only executable when:
% - F1 is not considered a floor,
% - F2 is considered a floor,
% - the lift is not located at F1,
% - F1 is above F2.
% When moving up happens, it makes the lift be located at F2.
% When moving up happens, it also makes the lift be located at F1.

% Moving down from floor F1 to floor F2 is only executable when:
% - F1 is not considered a floor,
% - F2 is considered a floor,
% - the lift is not located at F1,
% - F2 is above F1.
% When moving down happens, it makes the lift be located at F2.
% When moving down happens, it also makes the lift be located at F1.
