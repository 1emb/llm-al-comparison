There are two types of objects: "block" and "loc".
"loc" includes "block".

b1, b2, b3, b4, b5, b6, and b7 are blocks.
table is a loc.

Actions
pickupblock - picking up a block from the table.
putdownblock - putting a block down onto the table.
stackblock, block - stacking one block onto another block.
unstackblock, block - unstacking one block from on top of another block.
