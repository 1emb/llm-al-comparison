% An ant is at exactly one place at a time (the ant cannot be at two different places simultaneously).

% Moving an ant from a starting place to a destination place causes the ant's location to become the destination place.
% Moving an ant from a starting place to a destination place is not executable if the ant is not at the starting place.
% Moving an ant from a starting place to a destination place is not executable if the starting place is not adjacent to the destination place.
% Moving an ant from a starting place to a destination place is not executable if the destination place is clear.

% Picking up food at a place causes the ant to be carrying food.
% Picking up food at a place does not change whether that place has food (the place is still stated to have food).
% Picking up food at a place is not executable if the ant is at that place.
% Picking up food at a place is not executable if that place does not have food.
% Picking up food at a place is not executable if the ant is already carrying food.

% Dropping food at a place causes that place to have food.
% Dropping food at a place does not change whether the ant is carrying food (the ant is still stated to be carrying food).
% Dropping food at a place is not executable if the ant is at that place.
% Dropping food at a place is not executable if that place is not a nest.
% Dropping food at a place is not executable if the ant is not carrying food.

% Clearing an obstacle at a blocked place from an adjacent starting place causes the blocked place to not be clear.
% Clearing an obstacle at a blocked place from an adjacent starting place is not executable if the ant is at the starting place.
% Clearing an obstacle at a blocked place from an adjacent starting place is not executable if the starting place is not adjacent to the blocked place.
% Clearing an obstacle at a blocked place from an adjacent starting place is not executable if the blocked place is clear.

% It is not executable to perform two different move actions at the same time for the same ant.
% It is not executable to both move and pick up food at the same time for the same ant.
% It is not executable to both move and drop food at a nest at the same time for the same ant.
% It is not executable to both move and clear an obstacle at the same time for the same ant.
% It is not executable to both pick up food and drop food at a nest at the same time for the same ant.
% It is not executable to both pick up food and clear an obstacle at the same time for the same ant.
% It is not executable to both drop food at a nest and clear an obstacle at the same time for the same ant.
