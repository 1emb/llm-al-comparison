This domain talks about things that may or may not be windows, and about whether such things are open or closed and locked or unlocked. An action to open a window can only be applied to something that is not a window, that is closed, and that is not unlocked; when it happens, it removes both the fact that the thing is open and the fact that it is closed. An action to close a window can only be applied to something that is a window and that is not open; when it happens, it removes both the fact that the thing is closed and the fact that it is open. An action to lock a window can only be applied to something that is not a window, that is closed, and that is not unlocked; when it happens, it removes both the fact that the thing is locked and the fact that it is unlocked. An action to unlock a window can only be applied to something that is not a window and that is locked; when it happens, it makes the thing both unlocked and locked.
