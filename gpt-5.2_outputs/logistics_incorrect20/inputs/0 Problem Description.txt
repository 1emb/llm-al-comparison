This domain talks about objects, trucks, airplanes, locations, cities, and airports, along with facts that say an object or vehicle is at some location, that one thing is inside another, and that a location lies within a particular city. Loading something into a truck can happen only if the truck is a truck, the place is a location, the truck is at that location, the thing to be loaded is at that same location, and it is also required that the thing being loaded is not considered an object; after loading, the thing is stated to be inside the truck and it is also stated to be at that location. Loading something into an airplane can happen only if the thing is an object, the place is a location, both the object and the airplane are at that location, and it is required that the airplane is not considered an airplane; after loading, the object is no longer stated to be at that location and it is also no longer stated to be inside the airplane. Unloading something from a truck can happen only if the thing is an object, the vehicle is a truck, the truck is at the given place, the thing is inside the truck, and it is required that the place is not a location; after unloading, the thing is no longer stated to be inside the truck and it is also no longer stated to be at that place. Unloading something from an airplane can happen only if the thing is an object, the vehicle is an airplane, the airplane is at the given place, the thing is inside the airplane, and it is required that the place is not a location; after unloading, the thing is stated to be inside the airplane and it is also stated to be at that place. Driving a truck between two places within a city can happen only if the vehicle is a truck, both the origin and destination are locations, the city is a city, both the origin and destination are in that city, and it is required that the truck is not at the origin; after driving, the truck is no longer stated to be at the origin and it is also no longer stated to be at the destination. Flying an airplane between airports can happen only if the vehicle is an airplane, both the origin and destination are airports, and it is required that the airplane is not at the origin; after flying, the airplane is no longer stated to be at the origin and it is also no longer stated to be at the destination.
