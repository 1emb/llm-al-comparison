This domain concerns windows that can be open or closed and separately locked or unlocked. To open a window, it must be a window, it must currently be closed, and it must be unlocked; performing the opening makes it open and no longer closed. To close a window, it must be a window and it must currently be open; performing the closing makes it closed and no longer open. To lock a window, it must be a window, it must currently be closed, and it must be unlocked; performing the locking makes it locked and no longer unlocked. To unlock a window, it must be a window and it must currently be locked; performing the unlocking makes it unlocked and no longer locked.
