There are five types of things: "package", "truck", "airplane", "location", and "city".
"vehicle" is a common type for "truck" and "airplane".
"thing" is a common type for "package" and "vehicle".

Fluents
A (thing) being at a (location) is an inertial fluent (boolean).
A (package) being inside a (vehicle) is an inertial fluent (boolean).
A (location) being in a (city) is an inertial fluent (boolean).
A (location) being an airport is an inertial fluent (boolean).

Actions
Loading a (package) into a (truck) at a (location) is an action.
Loading a (package) into an (airplane) at a (location) is an action.
Unloading a (package) from a (truck) at a (location) is an action.
Unloading a (package) from an (airplane) at a (location) is an action.
Driving a (truck) from a (location) to a (location) in a (city) is an action.
Flying an (airplane) from a (location) to a (location) is an action.
