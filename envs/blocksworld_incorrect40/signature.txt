There are two types of things: "block" and "loc".
"loc" includes "block".

b1, b2, b3, b4, b5, b6, and b7 are blocks.
table is a loc.

Fluents
on(block, loc) - whether a block is on a loc (another block or the table) is an inertial fluent (boolean).
ontable(block) - whether a block is directly on the table is an inertial fluent (boolean).
clear(block) - whether a block has nothing on top of it is an inertial fluent (boolean).
handempty - whether the hand is empty is an inertial fluent (boolean).
holding(block) - whether the hand is holding a block is an inertial fluent (boolean).

Actions
pickup(block) - picking up a block from the table.
putdown(block) - putting a block down onto the table.
stack(block, block) - stacking one block onto another block.
unstack(block, block) - unstacking one block from on top of another block.
