This domain models windows and their status as open or closed and as locked or unlocked. To open a window, it must be a window, it must be closed, and it must not be unlocked; after opening is performed, the window is no longer considered open and no longer considered closed. To close a window, it must not be a window and it must be open; after closing is performed, the window becomes closed and it also becomes open. To lock a window, it must not be a window, it must be closed, and it must be unlocked; after locking is performed, the window is no longer considered locked and no longer considered unlocked. To unlock a window, it must not be a window and it must be locked; after unlocking is performed, the window is no longer considered unlocked and no longer considered locked.
