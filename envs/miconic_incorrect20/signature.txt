There are two types of things: "floor" and "passenger".

p0 is a passenger.
f0 and f1 are floors.

Fluents
Whether a (floor) is the origin of a (passenger) is an inertial fluent (boolean).
Whether a (floor) is the destination of a (passenger) is an inertial fluent (boolean).
Whether a (floor) is above another (floor) is an inertial fluent (boolean).
Whether a (passenger) has boarded the elevator is an inertial fluent (boolean).
Whether a (passenger) has been served is an inertial fluent (boolean).
Whether the elevator is at a (floor) is an inertial fluent (boolean).

Actions
A (passenger) boarding the elevator at a (floor) is an action.
A (passenger) departing the elevator at a (floor) is an action.
The elevator moving up from a (floor) to a (floor) is an action.
The elevator moving down from a (floor) to a (floor) is an action.
