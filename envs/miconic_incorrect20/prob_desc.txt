This domain models an elevator moving among floors and interacting with people who have an origin floor and a destination floor, with the elevator’s current location represented by which floor it is at and relative floor ordering captured by an “above” relation. A person can perform a boarding action at a floor only if that floor is a valid floor, the elevator is currently at that floor, and that person’s origin is that floor, and additionally the person is required not to be marked as a passenger; when this boarding action happens, it changes the state by removing the fact that the person is boarded. A person can depart at a floor only if that floor is a valid floor, the person is marked as a passenger, the elevator is not at that floor, the person’s destination is that floor, and the person is already marked as boarded; when departure happens, the person becomes boarded and also becomes served. The elevator can move up from one floor to another only if both are valid floors, the elevator is not at the first floor, and the first floor is above the second; after moving up, the elevator is no longer at the second floor and is no longer at the first floor. The elevator can move down from one floor to another only if both are valid floors, the elevator is not at the first floor, and the second floor is above the first; after moving down, the elevator becomes at the second floor and also becomes at the first floor.
