This domain talks about things that may be provinces or planets, a global condition called harmony that is either present or absent, a per-object condition called pain, and a two-object relation of craving between one thing and another. An attack can be carried out on something only if it currently is a province, is not a planet, and harmony is currently absent; when the attack happens, any pain on that target is removed, the target stops being a province, the target becomes a planet, and harmony becomes present. To succumb, the only requirement is that the chosen thing is not in pain; after succumbing, that thing is made not to be a province, harmony is removed, that thing is made not to be a planet, and pain on that thing is removed as well. To overcome, you choose an actor and another thing it is overcoming; this is allowed only if the second thing is not a province and the actor is not in pain; once it happens, harmony is removed, the actor is made not to be a province, any craving from the actor toward the second thing is removed, the second thing is also made not to be a province, and the actor is put into pain. To feast, you choose an actor and another thing; it can happen only if the actor does not crave the other thing, the actor is not a province, and harmony is present; after feasting, the actor’s pain is removed, the other thing is made not to be a province, the actor is made to crave the other thing, the actor is made not to be a province (again), and harmony is made present (again).
