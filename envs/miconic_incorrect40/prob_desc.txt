This domain models an elevator moving between floors and interacting with people who have an origin floor and a destination floor, with a notion of which floors are above others, whether someone is a passenger, whether they are boarded, whether they are served, and where the lift is located. The boarding action can occur at a floor where the lift is currently located and where the person’s origin is that same floor, but only if that floor is not considered a floor and the person is not considered a passenger; when boarding happens, it removes the condition that the person is boarded. The departing action can occur at a floor where the lift is currently located and where the person’s destination is that same floor, but only if that floor is not considered a floor, the person is a passenger, and the person is not boarded; when departing happens, it removes the condition that the person is boarded and also removes the condition that the person is served. The upward movement action can occur from one floor to another only if the starting location is not considered a floor, the destination is considered a floor, the lift is not at the starting location, and the starting location is above the destination; when this action happens, it makes the lift be at the destination and also makes the lift be at the starting location. The downward movement action is similar: it can occur only if the starting location is not considered a floor, the destination is considered a floor, the lift is not at the starting location, and the destination is above the starting location; when it happens, it makes the lift be at the destination and also makes the lift be at the starting location.
