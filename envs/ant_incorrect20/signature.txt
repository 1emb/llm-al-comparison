There are two types of things: "ant" and "place".

ant1 is an ant.
p1 and p2 are places.

Fluents
Whether an (ant) is at a (place) is an inertial fluent (boolean).
Whether a (place) is adjacent to another (place) is an inertial fluent (boolean).
Whether a (place) is clear is an inertial fluent (boolean).
Whether food is present at a (place) is an inertial fluent (boolean).
Whether an (ant) is carrying food is an inertial fluent (boolean).
Whether a (place) is a nest is an inertial fluent (boolean).

Actions
Moving an (ant) from a (place) to a (place) is an action.
An (ant) picking up food at a (place) is an action.
An (ant) dropping food at a (place) is an action.
An (ant) clearing an obstacle at a (place) from a (place) is an action.
