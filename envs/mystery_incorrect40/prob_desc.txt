This domain talks about things that may be provinces or planets, about whether harmony currently holds, about whether a particular thing is in pain, and about whether one thing craves another. An attack can be carried out on something only if it is a province, it is not a planet, and harmony holds; after the attack, that same thing is made to be in pain, it is (again) established as a province, it becomes a planet, and harmony is removed. To succumb with respect to something, the only requirement is that the thing is not in pain; after succumbing, the thing becomes a province, harmony is removed, the thing becomes a planet, and the thing is put into pain. To overcome, one thing acts with respect to another: it requires that the second thing is not a province and that the first thing is in pain; after overcoming, harmony is established, the first thing is (again) established as a province, any craving relation from the first thing toward the second is removed, the second thing is made not to be a province, and the first thing remains in pain. To feast, one thing acts with respect to another: it requires that the first does not crave the second, that the first is a province, and that harmony holds; after feasting, the first thing’s pain is removed, the second thing becomes a province, any craving relation from the first to the second is removed, the first thing ceases to be a province, and harmony is established.
